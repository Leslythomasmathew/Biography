Cristiano Ronaldo

One of the Greatest Football Players of All Time

Biography

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro was born on February 5 1985 in Madeira, Portugal. People think Cristiano Ronaldo is one of the football players ever. Cristiano Ronaldo started playing football with Sporting CP. Then he joined Manchester United in 2003. That is when people over the world started to notice how good Cristiano Ronaldo is at football and how many goals he can score.

Career Highlights

  • Cristiano Ronaldo has won the Ballon d'Or five times..
  • He has played for Sporting CP, Manchester United, Real Madrid, Juventus and Al Nassr.
  • Cristiano Ronaldo has scored than 900 goals in his career.
  • He has won the UEFA Champions League times.
  • Cristiano Ronaldo was the captain when Portugal won the UEFA Euro 2016.
  • Cristiano Ronaldo has set records in football.

Achievements

Cristiano Ronaldo has won league titles in England, Spain and Italy. Cristiano Ronaldo works hard and is very dedicated to being the best. This inspires a lot of people who like football.
